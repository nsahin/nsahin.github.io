Nil Sahin

I work as a computational scientist at Cyclica, optimizing the scientific platforms for drug discovery.

I am pursuing a PhD in the Department of Molecular Genetics at the University of Toronto. I am co-supervised by Prof. Brenda Andrews and Prof. Quaid Morris. My PhD thesis focuses on developing and implementing computer vision and machine learning approaches to analyze microscopy images to understand gene function.

I am passionate about implementing algorithms, building tools, learning new techniques, conducting research, working in teams. I am interested in applying my science knowledge to healthcare, with the aim to improve the quality of our lives and combat human diseases in the future.
